About LungScan AI

LungScan AI is a machine learning-powered web application designed to assist in the early detection of lung cancer using medical imaging. It utilizes histopathological images — microscopic images of stained tissue (commonly with Hematoxylin and Eosin or H&E) captured through a light microscope. These images allow for detailed analysis of cellular structures to detect cancerous changes. Users can upload an image and choose from various deep learning models (including VGG16, ResNet50, Inception, Xception, and a Custom CNN) to generate predictions. This tool is intended to support preliminary diagnosis and facilitate medical research.
